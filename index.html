Project Mondrian

A part of Web Development Portfolio by M. Rifqi Rahman


Embark on a visual journey through the essence of modern art with "Project Mondrian,". This project transcends mere aesthetics, embodying the harmonious interplay of color, line, and space that defines the iconic style of Piet Mondrian. Crafted solely with HTML and CSS, this digital masterpiece showcases the elegance of simplicity and the power of abstraction. Each element is meticulously designed to evoke a sense of balance and tranquility, inviting viewers to reflect on the beauty of geometric precision. The vibrant hues of red, blue, and yellow juxtaposed against a backdrop of serene white create an ambient atmosphere that captivates the senses. "Project Mondrian" is not just a visual representation; it is a celebration of creativity, innovation, and the timeless allure of art. Join me in exploring this magnificent tribute to one of the pioneers of abstract art, where technology meets artistry in a seamless blend of form and function.
